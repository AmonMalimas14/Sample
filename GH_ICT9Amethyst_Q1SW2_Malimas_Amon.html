Characters from Noli me Tangere
Image Characters Description
Crisóstomo Ibarra His full name is Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin, and a Filipino who studied in Europe. He also has a love interest towards Maria Clara. His father is named Don Rafael Ibarra but was deceased, and he changed his last name from Eibarramendia to Ibarra, from his ancestor's surname.
Kaptain Tiago He is a father that has a daughter which is María Clara. His full name is Don Santiago de los Santos, and he lives at Binondo.
Elías He is Ibarra's mysterious friend, a master boater, and a fugitive. He was reffered or known as "the pilot", he wants to revolutionize his country, and Ibarra's grandfather condemned his grandfather due to burning a warehouse, making Elias the fugitive that we all know.
María Clara Her full name is María Clara de los Santos, and she is Ibarra's sweetheart. She is also illegitimate daughter of Father Dámaso and Pía Alba.
Old Tasio He is a old man, where Ibarra seeks advice from. The town thinks that he is just a poor or just have bad luck, but inside he is actually quite wise/smart.
